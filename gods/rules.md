# Cards
- Power: Every card has a power value (usually from 1 to 5). The power value is not set for each cards but can be chosen by the player during "creation".
- Wonder: a wonder card stays in play when played, in front of the player who played it.
- Event: an event resolves its effect when played, then it goes into the discard pile of the player who played it.
- People: A people card has a condition. If matched, a player scores victory points equal to the power of that people.

# Rules

## Setup
Each player shuffles their 10 cards into a deck and draws 5 cards.
3 people cards start in the center, controlled by no one.

## Play
Players take turns one by one. On their turn a player can do 1 of the following.
- Play: active player plays a card from their hand (wonder or event)
- Pass: Active player draws a card from their deck. In case the deck is empty, the game ends immediately.
- End the game: No action or draw. The game is over at the end of the opponent's next turn.

Whenever a player meets the condition of a people card, that cards becomes controlled by them. In case of a tie, nothing happens.

## Winning
The end of the game is triggered by player actions (see End the game and Pass).
The player with most victory points at the end of the game wins.
In case of a tie, the player who triggered the end of the game loses.

## Keywords
- Destroy: When a people cards is destroyed, it just gets rotated 90 degrees. It stays in play but it does not score points.
- Restore: Whan a people is restored (from destroyed state), it gets rotate back straight and returns to normal state.
- +1 counter: A +1 counter on a card increases its power by 1. Multiple +1 counters add up.
